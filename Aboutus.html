SportCentra

Our Story

First founded in 2023 as a Danish startup, SportCentra is the industry-leading digital publishing platform; in 2023, the company moved its headquarters to the innovative hub of Manila, Phillippines. Today, more than 10M users worldwide use SportCentra to publish, convert, host, monetize, and share meaningful content across channels.

From enterprise marketers to individual creators, SportCentra user-friendly platform gives anyone the ability to put their message front and center with dynamic digital assets without sacrificing the aesthetic and tactile experience of print. With over 300,000 pages of content uploaded to SportCentra daily, readers worldwide get to discover, engage, and connect with content they truly love.
